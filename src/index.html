Memory Game

What's going on in this game?

The goal of the game is to remove all tiles from the board. They are removed by revealing 2 tiles, if the tiles are the same color, they are removed from the game, if not, they are covered back, so you have to remember where they were before.

At the beginning of the game, all colors tiles are shown for a moment.

During the game, you can use the help of small buttons at the bottom that reveal all tiles for a moment.

At the end of the game, when all tiles are removed, points are counted. The number of tries and the time of the game are important for scoring.
